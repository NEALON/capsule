WWAKE

Women’s Jewelry and Accessories

Brooklyn, New York, USA

WWAKE is a progressive jewelry line based in NYC.

Started in 2011, it has commanded the attention of industry leaders and tastemakers including Vogue, Vogue Italia, Refinery 29, Fashion magazine, Interview magazine, Glamour magazine, Allure magazine, and Lucky magazine.

It is currently collaborating with multiple designers and artists, including Assembly New York, Of/Kind, and Sight Unseen.
